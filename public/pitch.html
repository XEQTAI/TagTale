Every object

TagTale turns any physical object into a living social feed. Scan a QR tag, read the history, add your chapter.

Step 01
📷
Scan
Point your phone camera at any TagTale QR code. No app download required.
Step 02
📖
Discover
Read posts, photos and memories left by everyone who ever touched this object.
Step 03
✍️
Contribute
Add your own story — text, photo or video. Your post travels with the object forever.
Lives on the object
The feed is tied to the physical thing, not a person or account. Stories accumulate over years.
No app needed
Works in any mobile browser. Scan, read and post in under 30 seconds.
Follow objects, not people
Get notified when someone posts on an object you care about — a bike, a bench, a book.
Community moderated
Built-in reporting and AI moderation keep feeds respectful and spam-free.
