Problem & Solution
📌  Physical objects are socially silent
A vintage guitar, a city landmark, a shared tool — all carry rich human history with no way to surface it. QR codes today route to static pages or web links that expire.
💡  TagTale makes objects social
We turn any QR into a persistent, community-owned feed. Content is tied to the object — not a user, not a brand page. It compounds in value with every scan and post.
🔒  Moat: the content itself
Each tagged object builds a corpus of stories impossible to migrate. The longer it exists, the more valuable — and the harder to replicate.
Revenue Model
